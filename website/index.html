Sandboxed Previews

Deep Target Intel

We unpack redirect chains to expose the true destination, revealing domain registration age, server country of origin, and the full sequence of hops a link takes.

🧠

Swarm Intelligence

Benefit from a globally connected database. When the community flags a new phishing campaign, the entire network is instantly protected.

📷

QR Quishing Detection

Point your camera at any QR code and we decode it on-device before running the embedded URL through the full security pipeline — stopping QR phishing attacks before they start.

🔤

Homograph Attack Detection

We detect IDN homograph attacks where Cyrillic, Greek, or other Unicode characters impersonate Latin letters — so a fake "gооgle.com" (Cyrillic о) never fools you.

⚙️

Script-Level Analysis

We inspect every script loaded by the page to identify crypto miners, malicious CDNs, IP-hosted code, and excessive ad networks — surfacing risks invisible to a plain URL check.

The Mechanism

Four concurrent checks. One definitive verdict.

01

Capture

Type a URL, photograph printed text with OCR, scan a QR code with your camera, or share any link directly from another app.

02

Interrogate

Google Safe Browsing, sandboxed page analysis, domain age lookup, and our blacklist database all run simultaneously.

03

Correlate

Script behaviour, redirect chains, and domain spoofing signals are layered on top to catch threats that URL-only checks miss entirely.

04

Resolve

Receive a definitive verdict — Safe, Suspicious, or Malicious — with a screenshot, score, and full evidence breakdown.
